About Big Fish

Big Fish is a Chinese restaurant born in San Jose. We make wonderful Chinese food for this lovely community. Besides a lot of classic dishes like Chinese Chicken Salad, Mongolian Beef, Honey Walnut Prawn, Jajangmyeon (Korean Noodle), Jjamppong (Korean Seafood Noodle Soup), Tangsuyuk (Korean Sweet & Sour Pork/Beef), General Tso's Chicken, Orange Chicken, Peking Spareribs and Moo Shu series, we've dedicated to bring the most popular asian fusion food like dumplings, noodles to our customers.
