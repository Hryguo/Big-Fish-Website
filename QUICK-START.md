## 🚀 Next Steps (Easy!)

### 1. Download Images (10 minutes)
- Open **IMAGE-DOWNLOAD-GUIDE.md** for instructions
- Or just right-click images on your current site and save them
- Place them in the `images` folder with the correct names
- Use **IMAGE-SETUP-HELPER.html** to track your progress

### 2. Sign Up for Free Hosting (5 minutes)
- Go to https://www.netlify.com
- Click "Sign up" (free forever!)
- No credit card needed

### 3. Upload Your Site (2 minutes)
- In Netlify, click "Add new site" → "Deploy manually"
- Drag the entire `bigfish-website` folder into the upload area
- Wait 30 seconds for it to deploy
- Done! You'll get a free URL

### 4. Connect Your Domain (15 minutes)
- In Netlify, go to "Domain settings"
- Add your custom domain: bigfishchinesecafe.com
- Update DNS settings where you bought your domain
- Wait 15-60 minutes for DNS to propagate

### 5. Cancel Wix 💰
- Once everything works, cancel your Wix subscription
- Save $192-420 per year!

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Start with Netlify** - It's the easiest and it's free
2. **Test first** - Use the temporary Netlify URL to test everything
3. **Then connect domain** - Once you're happy, point your domain
4. **Keep backups** - Save a copy of the folder on your computer
